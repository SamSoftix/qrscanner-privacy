Overview

Your Privacy Matters

QR & Barcode Scanner ("the App") is a free Android application built to scan QR codes and barcodes, generate custom QR codes, and manage your scan history — entirely on your device.

Plain English Summary: The app works completely offline. Everything you scan is saved only on your phone. We have no servers, no accounts, and we never see your data.

This Privacy Policy explains what permissions the App requires, why it needs them, and how data is handled. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

Permissions

Why We Ask for Permissions

The App requests only the permissions it genuinely needs. Each is explained below:

  • Camera Required to scan QR codes and barcodes in real time. The camera feed is processed locally on your device. No images or video are ever stored or transmitted.
  • Flashlight (Torch) Used optionally to illuminate dark environments during scanning. You control it with the torch button in the scanner. Never activated without your action.
  • Internet Used only when you tap "Web Search" or "Open URL" to open a scanned link in your browser. The App itself makes no background network calls.
  • Read Media Images (Gallery) Requested only when you voluntarily choose to add a logo to your generated QR code. The App reads only the image you select; it does not browse your gallery.
Data Storage

Where Your Data Lives

All data created by the App — your scan history, favourites, and settings — is stored exclusively on your Android device using Android's built-in SharedPreferences storage system.

No cloud. No sync. No servers. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it as a CSV and share it yourself.

You can delete all stored data at any time by going to History → Delete All, or by going to Settings → Clear All History inside the App. Uninstalling the App also permanently removes all stored data.

Third Parties

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer your data to any third party — because we don't have your data in the first place.

The App contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics frameworks (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.), no crash reporting services, and no social media integrations.

The only external service your device may contact is:
